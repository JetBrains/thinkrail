rookery

Help casual birders log a sighting in under 10 seconds — the fastest, friendliest birding log.

Problem + added

Logging requires both hands, slow forms, and signal. By the time you're done, the bird's gone. eBird/Merlin assume you're a scientist or competitive lister. Notes loses sightings in a sea of grocery lists. The hobbyist who wants to remember, not document, gets punished.

Audience

Hobbyist birders, 30–60, weekend hikers. Not ornithologists, not competitive listers. The persona: someone currently logging in Notes and forgetting where.
