rookery

Help casual birders log sightings in under 10s — the fastest, friendliest birding log.

Why

Existing apps are checklist-heavy and treat every user like a hardcore lister. Casual birders abandon them. We want logging to be a single, joyful interaction.

Principles

  • One thumb, bad signal — every flow works on a phone in the field, offline-first.

  • Privacy by default — exact GPS never leaves the device unless the user opts in per sighting.

Out of scope (v1)

  • Social feeds, follows, comments.
  • Audio/photo ID.

webhooks

Receives provider callbacks (Stripe, PayPal). Translates them into internal ledger events.

Mini design

FastAPI router under /webhooks. Verifies signatures per-provider, persists raw payloads, then dispatches to ledger via in-process queue.

Functional requirements

  • Verify signature before doing anything else.
  • Idempotency by provider × event_id.
  • Replay-safe — duplicate events MUST NOT double-credit.

Non-functional

  • p95 < 200ms for accepted events.
  • Receipt ack within 10s of provider POST.

Testing

  • Unit: signature verification per provider.
  • Integration: replay 100 stored Stripe webhooks; assert idempotency.
  • E2E: full payment → webhook → ledger entry.
