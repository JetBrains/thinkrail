10 · Session Inbox

The fleet as a messaging app — a waiting-first list of every session in every workspace, one conversation in focus, context on demand. For monitoring many agents, a chat-style list beats a grid.
  1. The top bar earns its 34px: global session search (⌘K) and “New session” live in it.
  2. Every chat glanceable, waiting-first. Blocked sessions float to the top with what they need from you; the ◫ pin-to-split button docks a second conversation side-by-side.
  3. Projects become metadata. The workspace is a pill on each row — no permanent left panel spent on rarely-switched trees.
  4. Full-height terminal, one tap away: the slide-over’s Terminal tab gets the whole column, not a 40% strip.
  5. Files/Changes on demand from one predictable place — the context rail slides panels over the chat instead of squatting on width.
  6. Rows drag into splits: drop any inbox session on the conversation’s right half to open it alongside.

The one projection that collapses 1:1 to the phone (bottom tabs: Inbox · Files · Term).
